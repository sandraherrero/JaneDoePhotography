About me

I love to travel excessively, and to photograph where I travel. I love Haruki Murakami books beyond measure, and stalking other photographers online for inspiration. I love my dogs Emily, my cat Sybi, and my husband Ryan (in no particular order) (edit: add newest member of our family- Bjorn the handsome dog). I love a good film, and drinking coffee and eating biscuits (cookies). I love Astral Weeks, and being nostalgic for home (and Skype). I love editing photographs and getting that lump in my throat remembering the emotions I witnessed on the day of the photo. I love people's reactions to simple, happy photographs of their loved ones and am forever baffled by the power of an image. I love this lovely job and I would love to capture YOUR joy on your big (or small) day.
